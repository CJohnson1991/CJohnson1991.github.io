Our Vision

To show the world that new things should be scary. That even things they don’t understand can be fun and exciting and not something that should be avoided! To allow people to experience new and exciting things in a way that is enjoyable to all! Wrap It Up ventures to broaden the horizons of children and adults alike!

Humble Beginnings

Wrap It Up was an idea our owners grandmother would tell him on occasion. She had dreams of showing the world what it meant to grow and cook your own local produce and all the different and wonderful ways to prepare it! She lived part of her life very simply, meaning she provided for herself and others on a community farm and learned many ways for food preparation. Today her dream has been realized. We strictly use farm to table business’s and strive for excellence both in the produce we sell and the chefs whom we have preparing it for you, the customer!! We pride ourselves on our down home methods and cannot wait to show you all of our worldwide methods! We hope you enjoy his grandmothers cooking as much as he did as a kid!!
